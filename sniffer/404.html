Loot Not Found!

If you are seeing a Vercel error message while hosting on GitHub, your domain might still be pointing to Vercel's servers.

Quick Fixes:

  • Ensure you are visiting: https://yourusername.github.io/yourrepo/
  • If using a custom domain, check your DNS records at your domain provider (GoDaddy/Namecheap).
  • Make sure the domain points to GitHub's IP addresses, not Vercel.
